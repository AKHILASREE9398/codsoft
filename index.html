Project

The cosmetic industry continuously introduces new brands and products, primarily focusing on skincare and promising various benefits to consumers. While these products aim to enhance beauty and hygiene, they may also pose health risks due to their effects on different skin types and age groups. However, despite rigorous testing, there remains a need for comprehensive assessment of potential health risks associated with the use of these products. While cosmetics science is developing, its place in the industry and market share is increasing, yet it still has several negative effects on both human health and the environment. Cosmetic products are used by millions of people worldwide every day. While most cosmetics are safe, some products can pose health risks due to their effects on different skin types and age groups. This study addresses this issue by employing machine learning techniques, specifically logistic regression, on a database of cosmetic products .By analyzing factors such as customer skin type, age group, and product attributes, including ratings and reviews, the study aims to provide understanding of the health risks associated with cosmetic use This research endeavors to empower consumers with the knowledge to make informed decisions while advocating for safer practices within the cosmetic industry.
